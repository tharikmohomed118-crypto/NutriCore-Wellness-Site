ü•§ Smoothie

Breakfast - 250 kcal

Green Smoothie

Spinach, banana, protein powder

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 1 tbsp almond butter
  • 1 tsp honey
  • Ice cubes

Instructions

  1. Add spinach and almond milk to blender first
  2. Add frozen banana and protein powder
  3. Add almond butter and honey
  4. Blend on high for 60 seconds until smooth
  5. Add ice cubes and blend for another 30 seconds
  6. Pour into glass and serve immediately

Nutrition Information

Calories285
Protein25g
Carbohydrates32g
Fat8g
Fiber6g

üç´ Energy Balls

Snack - 150 kcal

No-Bake Energy Balls

Dates, nuts, and dark chocolate

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dates (pitted)
  • 1/2 cup almonds
  • 1/2 cup oats
  • 2 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1/4 cup dark chocolate chips
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients to a food processor.
  2. Pulse until a sticky dough forms.
  3. Roll dough into small balls.
  4. Refrigerate for 30 minutes to firm up.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
